Our Mission

Our Mission is to provide runner friendly races that are fun, offer variety, and great value. Our partners are typically smaller communities that benefit economically from bringing in runners from around the country.

Runner Friendly

You won't find friendlier, more helpful support anywhere. And most important, leave you with great memories.

After all, we want you to come back year after year!

Great Value

We combine reasonable pricing with high tech performance shirts, nice caps, and other thoughtful amenities.

Variety & Fun

From 10K to 6 Days, we offer a beautiful trail run, a road course, a stroll through the countryside, a party atmosphere, timed series of events on horse tracks and other loop courses, a record setting 100/50 miler, running in perfect conditions in a Dome; and one crazy hard test of your endurance and sanity.

Reach out today!
